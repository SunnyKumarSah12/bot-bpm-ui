Job Description: Front-End Developer (Angular / React.js)
Position: Front-End Developer
Location: [Remote / Onsite / Hybrid - City, Country]
Experience: 2 – 7 years
Employment Type: Full-Time

About the Role
We are seeking a skilled and passionate Front-End Developer with experience in modern JavaScript frameworks such as Angular and/or React.js. As a Front-End Developer, you will be responsible for building intuitive, responsive, and high-performance web interfaces that deliver exceptional user experiences.

You will work closely with back-end developers, UI/UX designers, and product managers to translate business requirements and design specifications into interactive and dynamic front-end solutions.

Key Responsibilities
Design, develop, and maintain responsive user interfaces using Angular (v2+) and/or React.js.

Convert wireframes and visual designs into interactive, high-quality front-end code.

Build reusable components and front-end libraries for future use.

Optimize applications for maximum speed and scalability across various devices and browsers.

Integrate front-end code with RESTful APIs and backend services.

Work with the UX team to ensure technical feasibility of designs.

Write clean, maintainable, and well-documented code.

Ensure cross-browser compatibility and adherence to accessibility standards (WCAG/ARIA).

Participate in code reviews, performance tuning, and front-end architecture discussions.

Collaborate in Agile teams, attend daily standups, sprint planning, and retrospectives.

Stay up-to-date with the latest trends, tools, and technologies in front-end development.

Required Skills and Qualifications
Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field (or equivalent practical experience).

2+ years of hands-on experience with Angular (2+), React.js, or both.

Strong proficiency in JavaScript, TypeScript, HTML5, and CSS3.

Experience with state management libraries like NgRx, Redux, Context API, or RxJS.

Solid understanding of component-based architecture and front-end design patterns.

Knowledge of responsive and adaptive design principles.

Experience working with RESTful APIs and integrating with backend services.

Familiarity with version control systems like Git.

Experience with testing frameworks such as Jasmine, Karma, React Testing Library, or Jest.

Ability to debug and troubleshoot performance and rendering issues.

Strong attention to detail and user-centric mindset.

Preferred Skills (Good to Have)
Experience with Next.js or Angular Universal for SSR (server-side rendering).

Familiarity with CI/CD tools and processes.

Knowledge of UI libraries such as Material UI, Bootstrap, Tailwind CSS, or PrimeNG.

Familiarity with GraphQL, WebSockets, or third-party integrations.

Basic understanding of accessibility and SEO best practices.

Exposure to design tools like Figma, Adobe XD, or Sketch.

What We Offer
Competitive salary with performance bonuses.

Remote and flexible working options.

A collaborative and innovative work culture.

Career growth and skill development opportunities.

Exposure to enterprise-level projects and modern tech stack.

Join us if you’re passionate about crafting great user experiences and bringing designs to life with beautiful, efficient code.
